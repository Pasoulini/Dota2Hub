    About Tiers

    [edit]

    Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 and Tier 4 are the tiers for the tournaments on this wiki. They are categorized as follows:

    NameDescription
    Tier 1Tier 1 tournaments feature the best teams from all over the world.
    Tier 2Tier 2 tournaments feature fewer top teams than Tier 1 tournaments..
    Tier 3Tier 3 tournaments have less prestige than Tier 2 tournaments but still draw a high level of competition.
    Tier 4Tier 4 tournaments are the lowest ranked events with no top teams participating.
